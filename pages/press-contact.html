Press & Contact

The jQuery Team is made up of many members, all of whom are more than happy to help answer any questions, concerns, or even thoughts about jQuery and the crazy place called the Internet. Below, you'll find a list you can use to contact members of the jQuery Team with all of your needs!

Do you have a question?

If you have a question, we encourage you to use the proper communication channels to reach out and we will do our best to assist you!

Stack Overflow

Stack overflow is the best place to ask questions and find support for all jQuery projects.

Matrix

jQuery chat is available on the Matrix network and can be found at https://matrix.to/#/#jquery_jquery:gitter.im.

Found a bug?

For reporting bugs in libraries, documentation, or content, the project's GitHub issues tracker should be used. All jQuery projects can be found at https://github.com/jquery
